How To Perform Some Common Bodyweight Exercises!


1. How To Do Push-ups


Muscles Involved: Arms, shoulders, chest, core


Technique: Put your feet and hands on the floor. Keep your body in a straight line without drooping in the middle. Your arms are the only body parts not keeping in a line. Keeping your core tight, push yourself away from the ground while keeping your shoulders close to your torso. Once your arms are fully extended, lower yourself back down to the ground to complete one repetition.



2. How To Do Squats


Muscles Involved: Lower body, mainly quadriceps and gluteus maximus


Technique: Stand with your arms out, perpendicular to your body. Position your feet roughly shoulder-width apart. Lower yourself into a crouch so that your hamstrings and calves touch. Then lift yourself back up, maintaining outstretched arms to complete one repetition.



3. How To Do Sit-ups


Muscles Involved: Abdominal muscles


Technique: Note that doing sit-ups on a hard floor may hurt your tailbone, so padding such as a mat may be helpful. Lie face-up with bent knees and feet flat on the ground. Cross your arms on your chest. Engaging your core, lift your back off the ground using your abdominal muscles to sit up. Lower yourself back to the ground to complete one repetition.



4. How To Do The Plank


Muscles Involved: Abdominal muscles, core muscles


Technique: Facing down on the ground, keep your body in a straight line, with your toes and forearms touching the ground and keeping you elevated. Engage your abdominal muscles and hold the position for a set amount of seconds (e.g., 10-60 seconds) depending on your ability.
