What is bodyweight exercise?


Bodyweight exercise is a type of strength training which uses the weight of your body to provide resistance against motion for the purpose of strength training and muscle growth, as opposed to using weights. Common examples of bodyweight exercises include push-ups, pull-ups, squats and sit-ups.


What are the benefits of bodyweight exercise?


  1. Bodyweight exercise is free; it does not require paying for equipment or a gym membership.

  2. Bodyweight exercise involves motions that use numerous muscles, meaning it can provide a full body workout quicker than weight training, which often targets a smaller number of muscles.

  3. Bodyweight exercise only requires your body and a small amount of space, meaning you can do it virtually anywhere and any time.

  4. Bodyweight exercises can be made as easy or as difficult as your skill level requires, so it is very accessible for beginners while still providing a challenge for seasoned veterans.
